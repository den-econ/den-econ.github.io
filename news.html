News & Events

Seminars, awards, grants, and lab announcements

2026

Seminar
April 2026
Public Seminar: Inequality Trends in Post-Pandemic Indonesia

Join us for a public seminar presenting new findings on income and consumption inequality dynamics in Indonesia since 2020. The seminar will be held at the Faculty of Economics and Business, Universitas Indonesia, and streamed online.

Speaker: Krisna Gupta & Budi Santoso
Venue: Room 201, FEB UI, Depok
Time: 14:00–16:00 WIB

Award
March 2026
Best Paper Award at ASEAN Economic Congress

Congratulations to Dinda Rachmania and Yudo Prabowo, whose paper "Carbon Pricing in a Developing Economy: Labour Market and Distributional Effects" received the Best Paper award at the 2026 ASEAN Economic Congress held in Singapore.

Grant
February 2026
New Research Grant from the World Bank

DEN Economics Lab has been awarded a World Bank research grant to study the impacts of digital financial inclusion in rural Indonesia. The project will run for three years and include randomised controlled trials in four provinces.

Publication
January 2026
New Working Paper on Minimum Wages and Informality

Dinda Rachmania and Yudo Prabowo have released a new working paper using bunching estimation to examine how minimum-wage hikes affect informal employment in Indonesia.

2025

Workshop
November 2025
Annual Lab Retreat & Methods Workshop

DEN Economics Lab held its third annual research retreat at Puncak, West Java. The two-day event featured presentations of ongoing projects and a methods workshop on synthetic control methods led by Arya Pratama.

Publication
September 2025
New Working Paper: Commodity Boom and Dutch Disease

Arya Pratama and Siti Aminah's working paper on the Indonesian commodity boom and Dutch disease effects on manufacturing has been posted to SSRN and is under review at the Journal of Development Economics.

Media
July 2025
Krisna Gupta in Kompas: "Rethinking Trade Policy"

Lab director Krisna Gupta was featured in Kompas newspaper discussing the implications of recent ASEAN trade agreements for Indonesian manufacturing and labour markets.

Conference
June 2025
Presentations at ASSA Annual Meeting

Three papers from DEN Economics Lab were presented at the ASSA Annual Meeting in San Francisco, covering trade, fiscal policy, and environmental economics.
