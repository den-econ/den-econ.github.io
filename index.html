About the Lab

The Denver Economics Lab is a research group studying how people, households, and institutions respond to economic policy. We draw on large administrative datasets, quasi-experimental methods, and structural models to answer questions with real‑world policy relevance.

Our work spans labor‑market responses to taxes and transfers, the economics of education and human capital, and development questions in low‑income settings. We are affiliated with the Department of Economics at the University of Denver.
