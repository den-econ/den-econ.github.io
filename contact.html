Frequently Asked Questions

Can I join the lab as a PhD student?

Yes! We accept PhD students through the official Universitas Indonesia doctoral programme. Contact us directly to discuss your research interests before applying.

Do you offer visiting researcher positions?

We welcome visiting researchers for periods of one to twelve months. Funding support may be available depending on the project. Please reach out with your CV and proposal.

How can I access your data?

Replication data for published papers is available on GitHub and data repositories. For access to restricted administrative data, please contact us with details of your research project.

Do you provide policy advice?

Lab members regularly engage with government agencies, international organisations, and NGOs. For formal consultancy or policy work, please use the contact form above.
